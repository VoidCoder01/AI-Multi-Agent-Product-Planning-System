📅 Task 2 — Chunking Pipeline
🎯 What

Split text into chunks

❓ Why

LLMs and embeddings work better with smaller text

📍 Where
utils/ → create chunking.py
⚙️ Implementation

Overlap: 100 tokens

  "text": "...",
  "source": "file.pdf"
}
